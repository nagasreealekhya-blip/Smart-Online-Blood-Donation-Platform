Frequently Asked Questions

How to register as a donor?

Click on the "Register" button, fill in your details, verify your email, and complete your profile. It takes less than 5 minutes.

How to request blood?

Navigate to "Find Donor" section, specify blood group and location, and submit your request. Donors in your area will be notified.

Who can donate blood?

Healthy individuals aged 18-60, weighing above 50kg, with no recent illnesses or medications. First-time donors are welcome!

Is donation safe?

Yes, all equipment is sterile and single-use. Medical professionals supervise each donation to ensure complete safety.
